About Our Blood Donation Initiative

LifeSaver is a non-profit organization dedicated to ensuring a stable blood supply for patients in need. Our network connects donors with hospitals and blood banks across the country.

Since our founding in 2010, we've facilitated over 1 million blood donations, saving countless lives. Our mission is to make blood donation simple, convenient, and rewarding.

We believe that no one should die due to lack of blood, and with your help, we can make this vision a reality.

The Donation Process

Donating blood is safe, simple, and saves lives. Here's how it works:

Registration

Complete a simple registration form with your basic information and medical history.

Health Check

A quick health screening including blood pressure, pulse, and hemoglobin level check.

Donation

The actual donation takes about 8-10 minutes. You'll donate approximately one pint of blood.

Refreshment

Enjoy snacks and drinks while you rest for 10-15 minutes before resuming your day.

Donor Eligibility

To donate blood, you need to meet these basic requirements:

  • Be in good general health and feeling well
  • Be at least 17 years old in most states (16 with parental consent in some states)
  • Weigh at least 110 pounds (additional weight requirements apply for donors 18 or younger)
  • Not have donated blood in the last 8 weeks (56 days)
  • Not have any infections or illnesses at the time of donation
  • Not have engaged in any high-risk activities that may affect blood safety

If you have questions about your eligibility, please contact us before coming to donate.
